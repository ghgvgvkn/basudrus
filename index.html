Bas Udrus — Free Study Partner Finder for Jordanian University Students

Bas Udrus is a free educational platform that connects university students in Jordan with study partners for their courses. Students at PSUT (Princess Sumaya University for Technology), JUST (Jordan University of Science and Technology), University of Jordan, German Jordanian University, Al-Ahliyya Amman University, and other Jordanian universities can find classmates, form study groups, get AI tutoring help, and improve their academic performance.

Our platform serves the Jordanian student community with tools for academic collaboration, exam preparation, course-based study matching, group study rooms, and AI-powered educational assistance. Bas Udrus is free for all students.
