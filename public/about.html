Common questions

Anything we missed? Email basudrusjo@gmail.com — a real human (the founder) replies within a day.

Is Bas Udrus really free?

Yes — the entire core product (matching, messaging, study rooms, help requests, AI tutor with daily quotas) is free for every Jordanian university student. The optional Pro tier ($5/month) only unlocks unlimited AI usage for students who hit the daily caps — see the pricing page for the full breakdown.

What does the Pro subscription actually unlock?

Three things: unlimited AI tutor / planner messages (no daily cap), longer AI context window (40k → 120k characters so you can paste an entire chapter), and priority response speed during exam weeks. That's it. No paywall on social features. Cancel anytime — every subscription comes with a 14-day full money-back guarantee.

Who's behind Bas Udrus?

Bas Udrus is built and operated from Amman, Jordan. The platform is run by a small team led by the founder, who responds personally to every support email at basudrusjo@gmail.com. We're not a venture-backed startup with an investor agenda — the platform is built sustainably from Pro subscriptions to keep the free tier free.

Is my data safe? Will my messages be used to train an AI?

No, never. Your messages stay on our infrastructure (Supabase, AWS Tokyo) and are not used to train any AI model. When you talk to our AI tutor, prompts are sent to Anthropic Claude under their zero-retention contract — they don't store or train on prompts either. We don't sell data, don't use ad networks, and don't run trackers. See the full Privacy Policy.

How are you handling payments?

Pro subscriptions are processed by an established Merchant of Record (currently Paddle, with Stripe as a fallback during onboarding). They handle payment collection, tax (VAT/sales tax), invoicing, and chargebacks. Your card details never touch our servers. See the Refund Policy for the full refund process.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from the billing portal in two clicks. Your access stays active until the end of the paid month, then drops back to Free. No retention calls, no "wait, are you sure?" pop-ups, no dark patterns.

What about refunds?

Every new Pro subscription comes with a 14-day no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. After 14 days we refund duplicate charges, billing errors, and extended outages. EU/UK residents retain the full 14-day statutory right of withdrawal independently. Email basudrusjo@gmail.com and we respond within 2 working days. Full Refund Policy.

Is this a dating app?

Absolutely not. Bas Udrus is strictly an academic study-partner platform. Soliciting romantic or commercial relationships is a banned use, and we suspend accounts that try. Match scores reflect study compatibility — chronotype, focus length, exam-prep style — not romantic interest. See our Terms of Service §4.

How do you keep students safe?

Every profile is moderated. Every user can block or report another user from their card — reports go to a human (currently the founder) and are reviewed within 48 hours. We use Row-Level Security on every database table so users can only see what they're allowed to see. Account deletion is honoured within 30 days. We notify of any data breach within 72 hours per GDPR.

Can I use Bas Udrus if I'm not in Jordan?

You can sign up and browse, but matching is currently optimised for Jordanian university courses. Expansion to other regions is on the roadmap. If you're studying abroad as a Jordanian student or you'd like us to support your university, drop us a line.

How do I contact support?

Email basudrusjo@gmail.com — that goes straight to the founder. Average response time is well under 24 hours. We don't have a phone line yet (small team), but every email gets a real human reply.
